About Me

I am currently completing my Bsc in Computer Science at the University of Sussex, near Brighton. I am a passionate Web and JavaScript developer and a general tech enthusiast. To pay the bills whilst at uni, I took up bartending and now make cocktails for a living at TGI Fridays, Brighton Marina. Outside of work my hobbies include watching films, playing games and listening podcasts including 'Reply All' and 'Stuff You Should Know'.

Portfolio

Portfolio

FoodSmarter

My submission for the final year project at the University of Sussex. FoodSmarter is a web application designed for mobile aiming to aid in the reduction of domestic food wastage for young professionals. Using a Google Firebase real-time backend alongside a vanilla HTML, CSS and JS frontend to visualise user's food stocks and suggest efficient food recipes to avoid food waste.

Locate A Mate

My team's award winning submission for HackSussex 2018. A service aiming to connect shy users with drinking partners using localised chatrooms. Managing a small team of 5 to create this webapp within 24 hours using geolocation, Twilio's web-chat API and Node.JS to win the 'Best User Experience' award.

Mixologist

Originally a prototype submitted for my year 3 module 'Technology Enhanced Learning Environments' at the University of Sussex, this mixology learning-platform concept was further developed to be useable on the bar as a webapp. Using the Web Speech API alongside a self-made RESTful drink monitoring API to measure and promote the accuracy of user's mixology skills.
